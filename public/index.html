About AI Image Editor

This application uses advanced AI models to edit specific objects in your images while preserving everything else - including background, colors, lighting, and style.

Features:

  • ✨ Precise object-specific editing
  • 🎨 Style preservation
  • 🔄 Multiple AI provider support
  • 💾 Easy download of results
  • 🚀 Fast cloud-based processing

Supported AI Providers:

  • OpenAI: DALL-E image editing
  • Stability AI: Stable Diffusion inpainting

How to Use

  1. Upload an Image

    Click the upload area or drag and drop an image (JPEG, PNG, or WEBP format, max 10MB)

  2. Describe Your Edit

    Type a clear prompt specifying which object to edit and how. Be specific!

    Examples:
    • "Change the blue car to a red sports car"
    • "Replace the wooden chair with a modern black chair"
    • "Make the woman's dress green instead of red"

  3. Click Edit

    The AI will process your image (10-30 seconds) and show the result

  4. Download Your Result

    Happy with the result? Download it! Want changes? Edit again!

💡 Pro Tip: The more specific your prompt, the better the results. Mention colors, styles, and details!
